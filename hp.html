Human Practices

Understanding the broader impact of ARGUS-2440: ethics, sustainability, and collaboration with society.

Our Approach

Integrated Practices

We continuously adapt our project through stakeholder engagement. Farmers, policy experts, and industry voices help refine our designs to ensure they solve real-world challenges.

Education

Outreach and education are core to our work. From interactive workshops with students to public talks on synthetic biology, we strive to make science accessible and engaging.

Ethics & Safety

Responsible research means prioritizing safety and ethical considerations. We evaluate risks, ensure biosafety standards, and discuss the societal implications of our designs.

Community Moments

Human Practices is about dialogue and collaboration. Our gallery captures our interactions with diverse groups: from farmers in the field to policymakers and students in classrooms.

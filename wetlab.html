Welcome to the Wet Lab

Hands-on biology powering ARGUS-2440: experiments, breakthroughs, and discoveries.

Our wet lab is where design ideas become tangible experiments. From engineering microbes that regulate nitrate levels to refining genetic circuits through iterative testing, this space represents the heartbeat of our project. Every experiment we run brings us closer to sustainable agricultural solutions.

Our Workflow

Design

Our design stage begins with computational modeling and literature review. We map nitrogen pathways, predict circuit behavior, and carefully balance productivity with biosafety considerations. These blueprints set the foundation for everything that follows.

Build

In the lab, design transforms into DNA. We assemble plasmids, culture engineered strains, and optimize conditions for growth. Each construct is carefully validated to ensure accuracy before moving to testing.

Test

Validation is critical. Using fluorescence assays, gel electrophoresis, and growth experiments, we test whether circuits behave as intended. These results guide iterative improvements.

Learn

Data drives refinement. We analyze results, identify bottlenecks, and update designs. This iterative cycle ensures our circuits become more robust with each experiment.

Key Experiments

To validate our constructs, we carried out experiments that tested both the molecular integrity and functional behavior of engineered strains. From confirming cloning success to tracking biofilm formation, these experiments built the evidence base for ARGUS-2440.

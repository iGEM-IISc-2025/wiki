Lab Notebook

A chronological record of our team's experiments, results, and discoveries throughout the iGEM season.

This week, the team convened to finalize our project idea. After several brainstorming sessions, we decided to focus on nitrate leaching in agriculture. Initial literature review was assigned to the entire team.

Key Activities:

  • Finalized project direction: ARGUS-2440.
  • Set up lab safety protocols.
  • Ordered initial primers and reagents.

The dry lab team began designing the genetic circuit using Benchling. The wet lab team prepared competent cells and started growing cultures of our chassis, *P. putida*.

Successfully performed Gibson assembly to construct our main plasmid. Transformed the construct into *E. coli* DH5-alpha for initial verification and amplification.
